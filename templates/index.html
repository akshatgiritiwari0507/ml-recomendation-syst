AI-Powered Antibiotic Stewardship Tool

Revolutionizing antibiotic use to combat bacterial resistance

Project Implementation

Our AI-powered Antibiotic Stewardship Tool was designed to address the growing challenge of antibiotic resistance by leveraging cutting-edge technology and comprehensive data sources. Below, we outline the detailed steps taken to implement this innovative solution:

Data Sources

We utilized a variety of reliable and authoritative data sources to ensure the accuracy and comprehensiveness of our model. These included:

WHO GLASS

We used data from the WHO Global Antimicrobial Resistance and Use Surveillance System (GLASS) to analyze global resistance trends.

OpenFDA

Integrated FDA drug databases to access information on recent antibiotic approvals, adverse events, and safety profiles.

RxNorm

Utilized RxNorm data to ensure standardization of antibiotic names, formulations, and dosages across all datasets.

Model Training and Development

The development of the predictive model involved rigorous methodologies to ensure accuracy and reliability. Key steps included:

  1. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing: Ensured data uniformity, addressed missing values, and harmonized sources for consistency.
  2. Feature Engineering: Included patient demographics, antibiotic usage, infection details, and local resistance patterns.
  3. Model Selection: Leveraged Gradient Boosting and Neural Networks for predictive analytics.
  4. Training and Validation: Used robust cross-validation and an 80/20 data split for training and testing.
  5. Explainability: Integrated SHAP values for transparent clinician insights into model outputs.

Deliverables

Functional Prototype

A fully functional prototype of the AI-powered Antibiotic Stewardship Tool, including prediction and recommendation capabilities, has been developed and rigorously tested.

Clinician Dashboard

We designed an intuitive, user-friendly dashboard for clinicians to input data, review recommendations, and provide feedback for continuous improvement.

Documentation and Training

Comprehensive documentation and training materials were prepared to ensure effective adoption and optimal use of the tool in various clinical settings.
