About This Project

Description: This innovative project addresses the critical global challenge of antibiotic resistance by leveraging the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI). By utilizing patient-specific data and real-time analytics, our solution enables doctors to make informed decisions for personalized treatments. It aims to revolutionize antibiotic prescriptions, reducing ineffective treatments and fostering better antibiotic stewardship.

Key Features:

  • AI-driven Disease Prediction: Analyze symptoms and medical history for accurate predictions.
  • Real-time Symptom Tracking: Dynamic adjustments to treatment plans.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored medication and precautionary advice for each patient.
  • Antibiotic Resistance Alerts: Early detection and prevention of multidrug-resistant organisms (MDROs).
  • Interactive Patient Dashboard: Empower patients with insights into their treatment progress.
  • Global Data Integration: Synchronization with international health databases for up-to-date recommendations.

Technology Stack:

  • Machine Learning models for predictive analysis
  • Integration with global health databases (e.g., WHO GLASS, OpenFDA, RxNorm)
  • Real-time Electronic Health Record (EHR) processing
  • Flask framework for a robust backend
  • Bootstrap and advanced CSS for a responsive frontend

Innovative Additions:

  • Telemedicine Integration: Enable remote consultations and instant treatment adjustments.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Simplify medical data interpretation for non-technical users.
  • Health Behavior Analytics: Provide lifestyle recommendations to complement treatments.
  • Gamification: Encourage patients to adhere to treatments through reward-based progress tracking.

Challenges Addressed:

Antibiotic resistance remains a pressing global health crisis. This project combats the issue by promoting personalized medicine, reducing hospital-acquired infections, and optimizing antibiotic usage. Our solution ensures a balance between effective treatments and sustainable healthcare practices.

Team:

Team SG3000 comprises skilled professionals passionate about leveraging technology for better healthcare outcomes. Our mission is to improve global health systems and address the growing challenge of antibiotic resistance.

Technologies Used:

Flask, AI/ML models, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and advanced data integration tools.
